Project Indago : Sprint 9 - Retrospective 16/09/2020

What Went Well?

  • Good level of challenge back from Devs/Test on Requirements and Design (Team 1)

  • Team progressed well given the limited team availability, planned for this (Team 1)

  • Allow more time for a in team retrospective (Team 1)

  • Good Development progress, with team members on leave (Team 2)

  • Easy to get answers on tickets from BA/design (Team 2)

  • Good communication on tickets (Team 2)

  • A lot of functionality developed (Team 3/4)

  • Good communication on getting things unblocked (Team 3/4)

  • Brainstorming sessions (Team 3/4)

  • Regular team meetings (CC)

  • Blockers/problems managed well (CC)

  • Demos have provided good feedback (CC)

What can be improved?

  • Overly optimistic with number of tickets brought into sprint - keep aware in sprint planing (especially when SIT is being carried out) (Team 1)

  • Various tickets assigned to client developer making it difficult to focus on one task - need keep more foresight (Team 1)

  • Various task (bug fixes, new tickets, clarification) - not clear what the priority is, set clearer understanding of priorities (e.g. S4) (Team 1)

  • A lot of traffic from bugs, people tagged on tickets not applicable to them, stops focus on other tasks (Team 1)

  • Test case steps weren't always clear (Team 1)

  • Developer training would of been better over 1.5 days rather than 3 half days (Team 1)

  • SIT Bug tickets coming in one by one would be better in bulk (Team 2)

  • Clearer Bug reproduction steps on Bugs (especially data driven processes) (Team 2)

  • Splitting functionality up into multiple tickets (Team 3/4)

  • Have more demos (CC)

  • Get technical prerequisites over to client PO and TL in advance (CC)
