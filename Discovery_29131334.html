Project Indago : Discovery

Created by Ryan Owens, last modified on Apr 30, 2020

During discovery we set out achieve the following:

  • Identify and Mitigate any Issues before Phase 1  (across Platform, People, Process, Portfolio)

  • Create a working Team and delivery Process that will be efficient in Phase 1  (Process, People)

  • Get Infrastructure agreed, designed and Up and Running (Platform)

  • Get Requirements process and tools fully worked out, Start refinement of requirements and inventory of widgets (Process and Platform)

  • Get UX Design and Delivery clarified, and ready to go. Assessing needs for custom components and plan their delivery (Portfolio, People, Process)

  • Create a Solution Architecture for entire scope (Portfolio)

  • PoC (tech app + funct app) activities to “learn by doing” hitting problems early (All Ps)

  • Plan Phase 1 Delivery with a refined and more detailed estimate (Portfolio, Process, People)

  • Establish Management, Reporting and Security processes

What Went Well

  • Willingness to work, and move everything going forward

  • The format and information delivered in the enablement sessions

  • Developer check ins

  • How we adapted the plan in order to the Covid 19 situation

  • Collaboration between the different organisations

  • Mendix support for the developers

  • Development for the form 19 was meet with good enthusiasm for first time building an app.

  • Use of screen sharing to troubleshoot development

  • Support from the test team (i.e. setting up test data)

  • Knowledge of the subject/product, knowing what the user want

  • Appetite for innovation

  • Adaption to the tooling (e.g. Jira)

  • Active support from PO’s and BA’s

  • Can do attitude

What was lacking

  • Method of communicating quickly

  • Documenting decisions

  • Communication

  • Provision of documentation in advance

  • Use of phone calls

What do you long for going forward

  • Glossy and Whoa

  • Seeing the apps in officers hands

  • The delivery of advanced features
