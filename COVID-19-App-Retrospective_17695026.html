Project Indago : COVID-19 App Retrospective

Created by Tiarnan Donaghy on Apr 20, 2020

The purpose of the COVID-19 app was to provide the user with structured information around COVID-19. This was built under a tight time constraint provided by the business and deployed in a live environment.

What Went Well

  • Speed at which we could show the business was a major win with the business.

  • On boarding of PSNI staff that were able to use the App (ease and ability for us to be able to update and use the app – creating and uploading more content).

  • Good learning space to build a react native app. Support from Chris and Oscar was good.

  • Impressed by how fast it was deployed via the client infrastructure.

  • Getting the initial requirement and capturing this a set of short user stories was very helpful to guide the development of the prototype.

  • Working together as a team – responsiveness from the team through email/call.

  • How we were able to manage this  in parallel with the project and didn’t completely stop the main project.

  • How we worked remotely.

  • Sprint Reviews for wider team kept everyone in the loop.

  • Got to see the reality of the tools (extent to which they work) e.g. the testing tool for react native app.

  • Urgency helped overcome technical challenges more quickly than they otherwise might have been.

What could we improve

  • How we move from development into Testing and move into deployment – criteria for exit, check lists, documentation etc.

  • For a bigger more complex piece of work need more governance / documentation / robustness of process

  • Chris’ best guess at development and testers best guess at what to test – if more complex would have got messy

  • Having access to devices is important to be able to test in the actual infrastructure – will have less chance of issues (e.g. security)

Sudden Death App: highlighted that the paper form wasn’t that well designed and therefore drove a lot more questions for the requirement. Thinking forward to the future Apps – unclear how well apps work (e.g. Vehicle recovery) and if in the current app they closely replicated paper forms and of the new requirements have fully addressed business process improvement.
